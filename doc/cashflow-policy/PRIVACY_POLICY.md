# Privacy Policy - CashFlow

**Effective Date:** February 3, 2026  
**Last Updated:** February 3, 2026

---

## Introduction

CashFlow ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in the CashFlow mobile application (the "App").

**In short: We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device.**

---

## Information Collection and Use

### What We Do NOT Collect

- ❌ Personal information (name, email, phone number)
- ❌ Financial data (bank accounts, credit cards)
- ❌ Location data
- ❌ Device identifiers
- ❌ Usage analytics
- ❌ Crash reports
- ❌ Any form of tracking data

### What Stays on Your Device

All data you enter in CashFlow is stored **locally on your device** using Apple's SwiftData framework. This includes:

- Expenses and income records
- Budget information
- Categories and customizations
- Shared expense groups
- User settings and preferences

This data is:
- ✅ Never sent to our servers (we don't have servers)
- ✅ Never shared with third parties
- ✅ Never used for advertising
- ✅ Only accessible by you

---

## Data Storage

### Local Storage

All your financial data is stored in your device's local database using Apple's SwiftData. This data is protected by iOS security features and is only accessible by the CashFlow app.

### iCloud Backup (Optional - Pro Feature)

If you purchase CashFlow Pro and enable iCloud backup:
- Your data is encrypted and stored in **your personal iCloud account**
- Only you can access this data (we cannot)
- Subject to Apple's iCloud Terms and Privacy Policy
- You can disable this feature at any time in Settings

**We do not have access to your iCloud data.**

### App Group Storage (Widgets)

To display widgets on your Home Screen, CashFlow uses App Groups to share data between the main app and the widget extension. This data:
- Stays entirely on your device
- Is not accessible by other apps
- Is not sent to any servers
- Includes only summary information (totals, budget progress)

---

## Third-Party Services

### Apple Services

CashFlow uses the following Apple frameworks, which may collect data according to Apple's Privacy Policy:

**StoreKit 2** (In-App Purchases)
- Used for: Processing purchases of CashFlow Pro
- Data collected by Apple: Purchase transactions, payment information
- We receive: Anonymous transaction receipt (no personal info)
- Apple's Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

**WidgetKit** (Home Screen Widgets)
- Used for: Displaying financial summaries on Home Screen
- Data: Only local data from your device
- No data sent to servers

**SwiftData** (Data Persistence)
- Used for: Local storage of your financial records
- Data: Stored only on your device
- No cloud synchronization (unless you enable iCloud backup)

### No Other Third Parties

CashFlow does not integrate with:
- ❌ Analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
- ❌ Advertising networks
- ❌ Social media platforms
- ❌ Crash reporting services
- ❌ Any other third-party SDKs

## Children's Privacy

CashFlow does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is rated 4+ and is safe for all ages. Since we don't collect any data, there is no risk to children's privacy.

---

## Data Security

### Your Responsibility

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you are responsible for:
- Protecting your device with a passcode or biometric lock
- Maintaining physical security of your device
- Managing iOS backups (iCloud or iTunes)

### Our Measures

We implement security best practices:
- Use Apple's secure SwiftData framework
- No network communication (no data transmission)
- No user accounts or passwords to compromise
- Code follows Swift security guidelines

---

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access Your Data

All your data is in the app. You can:
- View all expenses, income, budgets in the app
- Export to CSV format (Pro feature)
- Delete individual records or all data

### Delete Your Data

You can delete your data in two ways:

**1. Within the App:**
- Swipe to delete individual items
- Settings → Reset → Delete all data

**2. Uninstall the App:**
- Deleting the app removes all local data
- If you enabled iCloud backup, delete from iCloud separately

### Opt-Out of iCloud Backup

If you're a Pro user:
- Settings → iCloud Backup → Toggle OFF
- This stops automatic backups
- Existing iCloud data can be deleted from iCloud settings
